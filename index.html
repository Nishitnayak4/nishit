What is Taarangan?

The eagerly awaited Annual Inter - Collegiate Cultural Festival of Thakur College of Science and Commerce (Autonomous) - 'Taarangan' proudly entered its 13th year embracing the new normal by adopting Covid-19 appropriate behaviour guidelines. Each year competitions at Taarangan have been the stage for contingents from different participating colleges of Mumbai. The format this year moved to an e-version partially, with the students dancing and swaying to various performances from their safe havens. "Taarangan - Bringing Stars to Life" tagged as #13bhimerabhi was celebrated on 28th, 29th and 30th January, 2021 including online and offline events.
